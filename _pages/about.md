I am **Yuguangping**, interested in computer systems, software engineering, and research-oriented tool building. My work focuses on making complex software easier to analyze, debug, optimize, and reproduce.

I care about systems that are not only technically sound, but also practical enough to be used in real development and research workflows. This homepage collects my projects, papers, notes, and contact information.

# News
- *2026.05*: Launched this personal homepage.
- *2026.05*: Started organizing FastFLoc materials, paper drafts, and reproducibility documents for public release.

# Research Interests
- **Software debugging and fault localization**: locating suspicious code regions efficiently and making diagnostic results easier to interpret.
- **Program analysis and tooling**: building tools that connect static/dynamic signals with practical developer workflows.
- **Computer systems and performance**: understanding how system behavior, runtime cost, and implementation details affect real applications.
- **Reproducible research**: maintaining runnable experiments, clear documentation, and structured artifacts for papers and projects.
