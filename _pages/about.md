# Yuguangping

Nice to meet you! I am **Yuguangping (余广平)**, currently a Ph.D. student at the **National University of Defense Technology (NUDT)**, supervised by **Prof. Chun Huang (黄春)**.

Before joining NUDT, I received my B.S. degree in **Information and Computing Science** from **Hunan Agricultural University (HUNAU)** in 2023. Public profiles from HUNAU describe my undergraduate academic record, mathematical modeling experience, and recommendation to NUDT for direct doctoral study.

# Research Interests

My current academic interests include:

- **Computer science and software systems**: practical methods for inspecting, debugging, maintaining, and reproducing complex software.
- **Program analysis and debugging**: techniques that identify suspicious code regions, explain diagnostic results, and connect analysis signals with real development workflows.
- **Mathematical modeling and scientific computing**: modeling, algorithms, and computational evidence for solving practical technical problems.
- **Reproducible research artifacts**: runnable experiments, clear documentation, and project structures that make research results easier to inspect and reuse.
