# Research

My interests are currently organized around the following directions:

## Computer Science and Software Systems

I am interested in practical methods that make complex software easier to inspect, debug, maintain, and reproduce.

## Program Analysis and Debugging

I am interested in techniques that help identify suspicious code regions, explain diagnostic results, and connect analysis signals with real development workflows.

## Mathematical Modeling and Scientific Computing

My undergraduate public profile and competition records show sustained experience in mathematical modeling. I am interested in using modeling, algorithms, and computational evidence to solve practical technical problems.

## Reproducible Research Artifacts

I care about runnable experiments, clear documentation, and project structures that make research results easier to inspect and reuse.

Specific research projects, papers, and artifacts will be linked here after they are ready for public release.
